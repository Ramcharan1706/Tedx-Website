Theme

Break The Loop

"Break The Loop" is a call to disrupt the expected, to challenge cycles of habit, systems of thought, and stories we've outgrown. Whether in technology, culture, or personal growth — this theme invites bold thinking that breaks away from inertia and builds anew.

Disruption

Question the status quo. Reimagine what's possible when routines are interrupted with intent.

Awareness

Recognize the loops — social, emotional, systemic — that shape our lives and limit our thinking.

Breakthrough

Step into the unknown. Growth often begins the moment you exit the cycle.

Dr. Deepak Mashru

Professor, National Forensic Sciences University

Speaker One

Dr. Deepak J. Mashru is a visionary educator and pioneer in forensic linguistics, serving at the National Forensic Sciences University under the Ministry of Home Affairs. With over two decades of transformative work, he has redefined how language, law, and technology converge to strengthen justice delivery. Honoured with national awards for teaching and pedagogical innovation, he is known for blending Bharatiya knowledge traditions with cutting-edge forensic applications. A director of a prestigious ICSSR project, he stands at the forefront of designing new educational and investigative frontiers, inspiring students, scholars, and citizens to see words as instruments of truth.

Dr. Divya Raj

MD, Neo Institute Of Medical Sciences

Speaker Two

Dr. Divya Raj is a pediatrician and the Managing Director of the Neo Institute of Medical Sciences. She has trained over 15,000 doctors with GSL and AIG Hospitals and enabled treatment for 1,000+ international patients. Bridging Swadeshi values with global opportunities, she pioneered India’s first equivalent MBBS program in Uzbekistan with an NMC-aligned curriculum. Her White Coat Mentor Initiative promotes advanced medical skilling, AI simulation, and global exam readiness. During Operation Ganga, she led student rescues from Ukraine, relocating them to continue their education. A visionary mentor, she empowers Indian doctors to thrive worldwide.

Hema Thakur

Author

Speaker 3

Hema Thakur is an academic editor, mentor, and author with nearly a decade of experience guiding researchers through scholarly publishing. Her expertise has been featured in platforms like The Times of India, Zee Business, and Scholarly Kitchen. A published poet, her book Musings in Music has earned glowing reviews for its lyrical depth. She has spoken at premier institutions including IIT, BHU, and Ashoka University, where her sessions are valued for clarity and relatability. Her work spans borders, from leading workshops in Puerto Rico and Colombia to exploring Chile’s folklore and cultural legacies. Through writing, mentorship, and global connection, she empowers scholars and storytellers to share knowledge that inspires change.

Dr. A M Reddy

Founder, Dr Care Homeopathy

Speaker 4

Dr. A M Reddy is a pioneering homeopathic physician, entrepreneur, and founder of the Positive Group of Clinics. With over two decades of practice, he has established 50+ branches of Dr Care Homeopathy and Dr Positive Dental across South India, offering safe and holistic healthcare. Passionate about autism care, he founded the Dr A M Reddy Autism Center in 2016, becoming the first in India to advance homeopathic immunotherapy for ASD and ADHD. His work has brought relief to hundreds of children and hope to countless families. Rooted in values of compassion and resilience, he continues to redefine alternative medicine with innovation, dedication, and a vision to make world-class holistic treatment accessible to all.

Dr. Sairam Atluri

Founder & CEO, Tulsi Therapeutics

Speaker 5

Dr. Sairam Atluri is an internationally recognized stem cell specialist with over 30 years of clinical and research experience in the U.S. and India. Author of 30+ peer-reviewed papers and pioneer of several world-first stem cell trials, he has advanced regenerative medicine for conditions once thought untreatable. During the COVID-19 crisis, he published one of the earliest global studies on stem cell therapy for patients unresponsive to conventional care. Returning to India, he founded Tulsi Therapeutics with a mission to produce world-class stem cells at 100-fold lower cost, making them accessible to all. In collaboration with NIMS, his team has developed breakthrough stem cell–exosome therapies showing promise against liver failure and beyond. A visionary leader, Dr. Atluri is shaping India into a global hub for regenerative medicine.

Ramya Parashar

COO, MiQ's Bangalore Center Of Excellence

Speaker 6

Ramya Parashar is the Chief Operating Officer at MiQ’s Bangalore Center of Excellence, where she drives strategy, operations, and culture for one of the company’s fastest-growing hubs. Coming from a non-technical background, she has redefined leadership in tech-heavy domains by embracing discomfort, risk, and continuous learning. Recognized among Forbes India’s W-Power: Self-made Women and AIM’s Top 25 GCC Heads for two consecutive years, she is celebrated for blending data-driven systems with people-centric leadership. At MiQ, she champions inclusive workplaces, innovation, and empowerment, inspiring teams to transform challenges into opportunities. Her journey reflects resilience, boundary-breaking, and a belief that empathy and agility fuel lasting impact.

Nithya Rajendran

Indian Classical Vocalist

Speaker 8

Nithya Rajendran is a dual Indian Classical vocalist, trained in both Hindustani and Carnatic traditions for over two decades. She is the founder of Music Vruksh, an initiative that explores the healing and mood-elevating powers of classical music through concerts, workshops, and classes. A multiple-time TEDx speaker, including at IIT Jodhpur and CMRIT Hyderabad, Nithya bridges tradition with contemporary relevance. Her work has been featured in Outlook, The Week, CNBC TV18, India Today TV, Thrive Global, and more. She also writes on music and wellness, and has been recognized among “Women in Music” achievers by The Score Magazine. As a high-grade panel artist with All India Radio, she has many acclaimed performances to her credit, both in India and internationally.
