About TED

TED is a global non-profit platform devoted to spreading ideas through powerful and inspiring talks. Founded in 1984 as a conference on Technology, Entertainment, and Design, TED now covers every topic that shapes our world—from science and innovation to culture and humanity. Through short, impactful talks, TED aims to ignite curiosity, inspire change, and promote ideas worth spreading.

  • Founded in 1984 in California, USA
  • Expanded to include TEDx events worldwide
  • Focuses on "ideas worth spreading" philosophy

About TEDx

TEDx is a grassroots initiative created in the spirit of TED’s mission. It brings the TED experience to local communities through independently organized events. At TEDx events, speakers and performers share unique perspectives, original ideas, and transformative stories—sparking meaningful conversations and connections. While TED provides the guidelines, each TEDx event is self-organized with a local vision.

  • "x" = independently organized TED event
  • Operates under free license from TED
  • Curated by local teams for community-driven ideas

About TEDxKPRIT

TEDxKPRIT is a student-driven initiative hosted by Kommuri Pratap Reddy Institute of Technology, dedicated to amplifying voices, ideas, and innovations within our campus and beyond. Rooted in the TED ethos, TEDxKPRIT aims to create a platform where thought leaders, change-makers, and inspiring individuals come together to share insights that challenge, inspire, and empower. Through this event, we strive to foster a culture of curiosity, creativity, and collective growth in our academic community.

  • Hosted annually by KPRIT’s student-faculty team
  • Showcases diverse talks on science, creativity, and innovation
  • Empowers youth to explore "ideas worth spreading"

Theme

Break The Loop

"Break The Loop" is a call to disrupt the expected, to challenge cycles of habit, systems of thought, and stories we've outgrown. Whether in technology, culture, or personal growth — this theme invites bold thinking that breaks away from inertia and builds anew.

Disruption

Question the status quo. Reimagine what's possible when routines are interrupted with intent.

Awareness

Recognize the loops — social, emotional, systemic — that shape our lives and limit our thinking.

Breakthrough

Step into the unknown. Growth often begins the moment you exit the cycle.
